Villager's Cities Union: The New Horizons [Part 1][Creation]

Hello, guys! This is the first massive update of my creation map, based on the Default Minecraft world - Villagers cities Union, called "The New Horizons". There are 2 whole new countries and rebuild of old territories. Let's take a look!

Map "Villagers cities Union " is a net of the towns and cities, connected with each other with railway system. This map based on default Minecraft world. Currently there are 3 countries on the map: Villagers cities Union(fully completed, but I plan to expand it), underground state-city Divergement and Intermarium(3 of 5 towns are ready for you to explore). 

Villagers cities Union:

Big confederation of 17 towns: Downtown, Orangewood, Savannahtown, South Savannahtown, Birchtown, Minetown, New port, Deserttown, Swamp village, Jungle gates, Taigatown, Deserttown, Wheattown, Grasslandtown, Grassy village and river town. 

Downtown city: Downtown is the capital of Villagers cities Union. In this update city was expanded and partly rebuilt. There are many buildings, port, skyscraper and lots of content to explore. Screenshots:

Orangewood: Orangewood is the capital of the Orangewood confederation inside VCU. In this update it was partly rebuilt and expanded. Some screenshots:

Explore other towns of VCU on the map. Flags of the VCU towns are located in Main Lobby, so you can navigate by checking flags.

Divergement: is the Underground state-city, combined with inhabited Underwater dungeon. Here North-South( lineAlready exists) and East-West line(in project) of the ThroughMinecraft railway are crossing. There are 2 passenger stations and Freight station for both lines and big inhabited area. Some screenshots:

Intermarium

Intermarium is small country, situated between two seas. You can go there by North-South railway from Tavrida and Divergement. For now 3 of 5 towns are finished. Intermarium is mostly agrarian state, so many fields can be found there. Shorts description of towns

Turtle Lagoon town: big town with port and other infrastructure.

Foxwood: small village, located in forest

Cliffanger: the capital of Intermarium. Beautiful and interesting town
