Vein-Miner and Tree Capitator Addon (New Settings Update)

This addon introduces a new way of mining ores. Now you can mine the whole vein of the ores by just mining one ore block. You just have to sneak by pressing the shift button or whatever button you have set for sneaking. By default, it's the left shift. This addon is survival-friendly too.

 

UPDATE : New Setting added to the addon which helps toggle between the vein mineable blocks.

You can mine ores and even their deep slate version. All the thing you have to do is sneak. But if you have to mine a single piece, just mine it normally, without sneaking.

You can mine all the ores including iron, copper, and many more. In the coming future, more blocks support will be added to this addon.

Currently, the blocks supported for vein mining are as follows :

 

 

 

  1. Ancient Debris
  2. Diamonds (Includes Deepslate Version)
  3. Iron (Includes Deepslate Version)
  4. Gold (Includes Deepslate Version)
  5. Copper (Includes Deepslate Version)
  6. Coal (Includes Deepslate Version)
  7. Redstone
  8. Lapis (Includes Deepslate Version)
  9. Emerald (Includes Deepslate Version)
  10. Nether Gold
  11. Nether Quartz
  12. Gravel
  13. Granite
  14. Andesite
  15. Diorite
  16. All Wood Logs
  17. Nether Wood
  18. Wart Blocks (Crimson and Warped)
  19. Glow Stone
  20. Clay

New Settings for Vein Miner

Now you can toggle between vein minable blocks with the new settings option in the addon.

This addon contains 5 settings :

 

1.Ores Only

At this settings you will able to vein mine only ores like iron ore, gold ore and other ores.

Total Vein minable ores are 

  1. Ancient Debris
  2. Diamonds (Includes Deepslate Version)
  3. Iron (Includes Deepslate Version)
  4. Gold (Includes Deepslate Version)
  5. Copper (Includes Deepslate Version)
  6. Coal (Includes Deepslate Version)
  7. Redstone
  8. Lapis (Includes Deepslate Version)
  9. Emerald (Includes Deepslate Version)
  10. Nether Gold
  11. Nether Quartz

 

2. Ores and Wood

At this settings you will be able to vein mine all the above mentioned ores as well as the wood logs which include oak wood, spruce wood, etc.

Total Vein Minable blocks are :

  1. Ancient Debris
  2. Diamonds (Includes Deepslate Version)
  3. Iron (Includes Deepslate Version)
  4. Gold (Includes Deepslate Version)
  5. Copper (Includes Deepslate Version)
  6. Coal (Includes Deepslate Version)
  7. Redstone
  8. Lapis (Includes Deepslate Version)
  9. Emerald (Includes Deepslate Version)
  10. Nether Gold
  11. Nether Quartz
  12. All Wood Logs(oak, spruce, birch, dark oak, jungle)
  13. Nether Wood Logs(Crimson Stem, Warped Stem)

 

3. Ores Wood and Stones

At this settings you will be able to vein mine all the above mentioned ores and wood logs as well as the stones which include diorite, andesite, and granite. (Deepslate is not included because it can cause extreme lag when in 1.18 deepslate caves)

Total Vein Minable blocks are :

  1. Ancient Debris
  2. Diamonds (Includes Deepslate Version)
  3. Iron (Includes Deepslate Version)
  4. Gold (Includes Deepslate Version)
  5. Copper (Includes Deepslate Version)
  6. Coal (Includes Deepslate Version)
  7. Redstone
  8. Lapis (Includes Deepslate Version)
  9. Emerald (Includes Deepslate Version)
  10. Nether Gold
  11. Nether Quartz
  12. All Wood Logs(oak, spruce, birch, dark oak, jungle)
  13. Nether Wood Logs(Crimson Stem, Warped Stem)
  14. Andesite
  15. Diorite
  16. Granite

 

4. All blocks excluding Gravel

At this settings you will be able to vein mine all the above mentioned ores, wood logs, stones as well as some miscellaneous blocks like clay block, wart blocks(Nether Wart and Warped Wart) and Glow Stone.

Total Vein Minable blocks are :

  1. Ancient Debris
  2. Diamonds (Includes Deepslate Version)
  3. Iron (Includes Deepslate Version)
  4. Gold (Includes Deepslate Version)
  5. Copper (Includes Deepslate Version)
  6. Coal (Includes Deepslate Version)
  7. Redstone
  8. Lapis (Includes Deepslate Version)
  9. Emerald (Includes Deepslate Version)
  10. Nether Gold
  11. Nether Quartz
  12. All Wood Logs(oak, spruce, birch, dark oak, jungle)
  13. Nether Wood Logs(Crimson Stem, Warped Stem)
  14. Andesite
  15. Diorite
  16. Granite
  17. Clay
  18. Wart Blocks (Nether Wart and Warped Wart)
  19. Glow Stone

 

5. All Blocks

At this settings you will be able to vein mine all the above mentioned ores, wood logs, stones, miscellaneous blocks, as well as Gravel.

Total Vein Minable blocks are :

  1. Ancient Debris
  2. Diamonds (Includes Deepslate Version)
  3. Iron (Includes Deepslate Version)
  4. Gold (Includes Deepslate Version)
  5. Copper (Includes Deepslate Version)
  6. Coal (Includes Deepslate Version)
  7. Redstone
  8. Lapis (Includes Deepslate Version)
  9. Emerald (Includes Deepslate Version)
  10. Nether Gold
  11. Nether Quartz
  12. All Wood Logs(oak, spruce, birch, dark oak, jungle)
  13. Nether Wood Logs(Crimson Stem, Warped Stem)
  14. Andesite
  15. Diorite
  16. Granite
  17. Clay
  18. Wart Blocks (Nether Wart and Warped Wart)
  19. Glow Stone
  20. Gravel

 

Note : Default Settings will be set to "All Blocks". You can change it using the settings tab of the addon.

 

NOTE: Fortune doesn't work while vein mining. To do so, you have to mine it normally.
