Custom Village MCPE 1.19.30

Hello everyone, this is a map of a custom village with farming areas, many medieval style houses, a port for ships and an addon that adds custom boxes. I hope you enjoy.

 

This is a medieval themed custom village which has many that are decorated for the interiors. There are several cultivation areas and a large port for boats.

There is an area for merchants. With this map you can play with your friends and have a lot of fun.

This map was created from 1.16.100 and was updated at 1.19.30 with the new terrain generation.
