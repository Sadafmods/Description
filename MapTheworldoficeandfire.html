The World of Ice and Fire

 

This is a survival map with more than 100 castles and cities across the continents of Westeros, Essos and Sothoryos from A Song of Ice and Fire. This is a Bedrock map converted from the Original Java map by Maester Renan.

This map features all the cities/towns/villages/ruins (more than 100 at the least only in Westeros, all 9 free cities of Essos and even the Ruins of Valyria and other ruins around the map) that are listed on the official maps of a Song Ice & Fire, including the continents of Essos and Sothoryos. You will start in the throne room of the Red Keep in King's Landing. 

The map was originally created by Maester Renan in 1.12.2 version of the game, and converted into Bedrock version by me and I also edited every single house banners in Westeros (the conversion made all the banner colors negative) and edited a few locations like Dragonstone, King's Landing, Castle Black, etc. The Map should work fine on any version from 1.17 and newer (I've used some Deepslate and Amethyst around the map)

You can support the creator by following him on his Instagram and Twitter. It took him over a year to complete the entire map so all the credit goes to him and the original map (Java Edition) can be found Here.

  • Other changes: Added some spawners around various locations

Installation

Simply rename the file to Ice and Fire.mcworld and open it with Minecraft, it should open the game and start importing the world.

Incase, you cannot open the file with Minecraft or it fails to import the world, then you can rename the file to .zip , extract it and move to world file to minecraftWorlds folder inside the game directory com.mojang
