Instant Bridge

 

This add-on adds instant bridges which are generated by interacting with the instant bridge block. You can also activate and deactivate them whenever you want by interacting with the instant bridge block.

 


You can craft instant bridges from all types of wood.

 

 

 

 

 

 

Crafting Recipes

All instant bridges have the same crafting, only the type of wood changes.


You can also find them in your inventory

To use them you just have to place it on the side of any block and then touch it (in mobile version) or right click. Instant Bridge has a distance of 64 blocks.

 

You can make chains of Instant Bridges infinitely.
