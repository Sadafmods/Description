Iron Golem Mode

Welcome to Iron Golem Mode for Minecraft Pocket/Bedrock Edition! This addon gives you the powers and the skin of an Iron Golem. 

Features: 

- Half a heart of fall damage taken when dropped from any height

- 100 health (or 5 rows of hearts)

- Ability to breathe underwater

- The skin of an Iron Golem

- 4 extra damage per hit

 

About Iron Golem Mode

Here I'll be showing everything that was listed in "Features". To begin, we have the half a heart of fall damage taken when dropped from any height. This means any fall damage you take, it will always only hurt half a heart. You could even drop from 1000 blocks up!

 

 

 

Next, if you didn't see the amount of health you get that was shown in the first image, here's a zoomed-in image of it. All Iron Golems have this much health in vanilla, meaning it's only fair you get it too!

Iron Golems can also breathe underwater in vanilla (although they do sink to the bottom and can't get back up). All that really changed here is the drowning bubbles being removed and the fact that you don't have to worry about drowning anymore.

And of course, you also get the skin of an Iron Golem. Now unlike my other addons where you stay in your same old skin, in this one you actually look like an Iron Golem and have the animations! This includes walking, crouching, swimming, etc.

The Iron Golem skin was not created by me, only the powers were. All I did was merge RuanV911's Custom Iron Golem with my addon. GO GIVE THEM CREDIT!!

Lastly, we have the 4 extra damage per hit. This can't really be shown through an image, but you get the point. Every time you hit something, an additional 4 damage will be added. So if you're attacking with a diamond sword then you'll be doing about 11 damage instead of the regular 7.

Installation

How to install Iron Golem Mode:

 

1. Click "MediaFire” in the Downloads section

2. Download irongolem.mcaddon once the page is loaded

3. Import the pack to Minecraft Pocket Edition or Bedrock Edition after downloading

4. Add both the behavior pack and resource pack to your world
