Golem Mobs Addon

What is Golem Mobs? : It is a complement that aims to add difficulty and variety of mobs, although it also adds new materials, with which new armor and tools can be built with a special use. The mobs it adds are Golems of the majority of vanilla humanoid mobs, that is, they are resistant enemies like Iron Golems, and most of them leave negative effects when attacking enemies.
