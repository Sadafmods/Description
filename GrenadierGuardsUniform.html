[3D] Queen's Guards Uniforms

This is a small uniform pack containing all of the summer uniforms for the Queen's Foot Guards. This includes the Coldstream Guards, Grenadier Guards, Irish Guards, Scots Guard, & Welsh Guard. 

Each regiment has a unique chest plate and helmet, with the corresponding button pattern on the shirt and cuffs, and the corresponding plume on the bearskin helmet. All the uniforms are 3D modelled and have matching leggings and boots. Here is a picture of the uniforms:
