SpongeBob Addon

SpongeBob is a sea sponge that surely everyone knows for his famous cartoon, in this addon add SpongeBob and his friends to our Minecraft. This addon is under development so it may have bugs and if you want to see any character tell me in the comments for the next update

friendly mobs

sponge bob

spongebob is a sea sponge who likes to cook crabburgers, fish for jellyfish and play with his friends in the mod he is friendly and being a spawn summons his pet gary on a leash.

  • health:35-45
  • attack:4

patrick

patrick star is a sloth who likes to eat krabby patties and fish with spongebob, spongebob's best friend

 

 

 

  • health:48-50
  • attack:6

gary

Gary is SpongeBob's pet who appears together with SpongeBob or separately.

  • health:20-23
  • attack:3

squidward

squidward is spongebob's grumpy neighbor who according to him hates his neighbors but has been seen worried about them in the mod he is friendly and fights monsters

  • health:40-45
  • attack:4

mr. crabs

don crab is a greedy and stingy boss of spongebob who works in the krusty crustacean that you may add in an update

  • health:44-48
  • attack:8

karen

Karen is Plankton's computer wife who is an ally of the player and will help fight the hostile mobs.

  • health:50-55
  • attack:5

plankton

plankton is the rival of mr crab but in the addon they are friends and do not attack each other

  • health:23-28
  • attack:1

hostile mobs

toybob

toybob is a robot based on spongebob created by plankton in the christmas special in the series, this mob has the ability to attack from a distance by shooting fireballs at a specific distance and is able to beat the warden.

  • health:320 (regeneration 1 and resistance 1)
