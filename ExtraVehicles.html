Extra Vehicles Addons

Introducing the "Extra Vehicles Addon" for Minecraft Bedrock! This amazing add-on brings a new dimension to your Minecraft experience with its realistic and detailed big Horse Wagons. This powerful wagon can carry chests, giving you extra inventory space to transport your goods across the world. The wagon can move at different speeds and comes with a lantern attached to it for lighting up the area during dark times.

The realistic animations of the horses and wagon will give you the feeling of riding a real horse-drawn carriage, while the well-designed UI will ensure that you have complete control over the wagon's movements. The wheels generate dust particles when moving, giving it a realistic touch. The 3D model of the wagon is detailed and well-textured, making it a visual treat to watch.

Moreover, this add-on has a lot of high-quality realistic sounds that will immerse you in the game. Though currently, only LVL3 Horse Wagon is available, LVL1 and LVL2 are in the works and will be available soon.

 

 

Storage System

How to add a chest:

a) Bring a chests and hold it on your hand

b) press on the wagon while holding the chest (without sneaking)

c) You can add up-to 14 chest in the one Wagon

 

How to remove a chest:

a) Don't hold any thing in your hand

b) Sneak and press on the wagon

 

Notice:

- Don't remove any chest from the wagon if there is any item on the last 10 slots.

- if you did so, the last 10 slots will be deleted as well as the items inside this slots and you will not be able to restore the lost items again.

Speed System

Description:

There is 6 speed levels in total. You can increase the wagon speed level by (right clicking/pressing button) while riding the wagon and moving.

When you increase the speed the Rope Wiping animation will be played.

 

Estimated Speeds in m/s:

player Sprinting = 6 m/s

Speed 1 = 0.5 m/s

Speed 2 = 1.0 m/s

Speed 3 = 2.9 m/s

Speed 4 = 5.8 m/s

Speed 5 = 8.8 m/s

Speed 6 = 11.7 m/s

 

Notice:

-The Timer purpose is to decrease the Wagon speed by time (It is as if the horses slowing with time).

- Every time you increase speed a timer of 5s will start.

 

- whenever the timer runs out, It reduce the speed level by -1.

- To reset the timer, whip the horses to increase the speed and reset the timer back.

- There is no timer running, when you are on speed 1.

 

Tip:

Spam on Wiping the horses when you reach speed 6, so that you maintain the same speed without any decrease in speed.
