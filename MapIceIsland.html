Minecraft Small Ice Island Map

 

This is an ice island map. The Ice Island map will have ice and houses around it. There will be a big hill in the middle of it. Some homes square measure huge and a few homes square measure little. The island is surrounded by sea. This map is for Pocket Edition and Bedrock Edition. In addition to version 1.19 will support different versions.

Ice Island 

There are many types of houses, big and small, on this island. There are many beautiful places inside the house.

Structure

There is a large ship on this map. This ship is located very close to the island. There is a big mountain on this island. There is a cave under that mountain. You will see a beautiful place next to the cave.
