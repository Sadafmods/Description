Well here I bring you "Flappy Bird Craft" For Minecraft Bedrock. 

Flappy Bird Craft is a flappy bird minigame within Minecraft. 

Where you can play like the original game, what are you waiting for to beat your records?

In order to play you just need to enter the portal that is in the Lobby.

From there you will already be in a game, to start you must press the Skip button and the Game will begin.

You must press the jump button several times so that you can avoid the tonos that will appear throughout the game, which are completely random, you will not have a game the same as the previous one.

 

 

 

If you lead to death, they will give you 2 items that are to go out to the lobby and another that is to restart the game.

In case the map gets bugged, open a button behind it that restarts the map, so the Bugs will be solved.
