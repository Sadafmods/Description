The Laboratory

Complete many assorted tasks, all themed around the core mechanisms of Minecraft; from brewing to crafting to parkour, lots of Minecraft skills will be needed to progress through the tasks. 

There are 11 challenges in total, each being themed around a certain element of Minecraft. As there are no mods, all done in vanilla, this map requires good Minecraft knowledge to be played well.

Also, each challenge will use materials from previous challenges to be completed, so make sure to use your materials wisely and know the most efficient crafting recipes and smelting times!
