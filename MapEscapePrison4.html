Escape From The Prison 4

The police arrest you because you look like a criminal. You get a life sentence, what will you do? Will you stay there or will you make an escape plan to escape? Luckily the cops take a winter vacation. Good luck!

==============================================================

Map description:

This prison is a bit bigger than the others you were in, the cops lock several doors and they can only be unlocked with a keycard. I found out that they have locked the double cells, the solitarys, the kitchen, the security room and the front door. There is a large hallway on the second floor with the interrogation room, laundry room, and showers. You can go straight to the police station, which is where the exit is located, through the vents.
