City of New Greens

 

Welcome to the City of New Greens! A great modern city with good structures and a great greenery in between the structures that makes the city so unique to others. The city was great for roleplay so the city was definitely worth it. 

New Greens is a modern city filled with greenery, a large roads and streets, and good structures and buildings that you have seen in those Minecraft cities that exists here. You want to see what the city looks like? Here are the pictures :

This is what New Greens looks like. 

It has some basic needs of a city like bank, hospital and police station. 

A school and the city hall. 

Residential houses in the city. 

A modern subdivision, a large bank, a cimena and a water park. 

Fastfood chains like KFC, a small Ikea furniture store, a convenience store like 7-11, and famous buildings in the world like One World Trade Center and Empire State Building with Willis Tower and Taipei 101.

Capcom Spotlight March 2023

 

Company buildings like Samsung, Mojang Studios, Microsoft and Apple Inc. Also, there's Lovely Inc. from Stampy's Lovely World. 

A swimming center, an arena like boxing arena, and some buildings like the Google HQ building. 

A lovely church. 

A TV station namely NBS. 

And so much more to see... 

So, what are you waiting for? You can download the map now and explore it for yourself to see the wonder of modern city of New Greens.
