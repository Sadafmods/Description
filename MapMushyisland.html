Mushy Island [2.0]

Mushy Island (Creation) is a wonderful island full of giant mushrooms and Nordic houses, as well as a ship and a mountain fortress. Explore this amazing island and have fun! This map was created by Toii and ported by Voxed Studio to the Bedrock Edition.

Mushy Island is a perfect creation map for you to play survival. You can play alone or call your friends to accompany you on your adventures!
