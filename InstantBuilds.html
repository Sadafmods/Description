Instant Builds Add-On - [Updated] Now Over 20 Medieval Structures

Too lazy to build a hut? Maybe, maybe not. Too lazy to build a castle? I sure am, I bet you are too. You need this pack! We've got instant huts, houses, small taverns, massive taverns, fighting pits, small castles, huge castles... and a whole lot more! Including two types of serious.... TROUBLE.

Updated to now include an enchantment room, massive tavern, an even bigger castle, a new placement system, and a whole lot more:

 

 

 

 

Essentially you craft your instant spawn egg, drop it down, and stand back. In a couple of seconds you'll have your instant building, ready to move in to!

Placement System

Your new building will spawn in front-left of you. To help you out with this, when you place your building down you will see some helpful arrows pointing in front and to the left of the little structure, like this:

You will hear it count down from four. If they're not pointing in the directions you want, kill that hut and your spawn egg will drop so you can have another go. The arrows aren't 100% accurate and your results may vary. Always try to face one of the cardinal directions (North, South, East or West). Practice in Creative until you're confident!
